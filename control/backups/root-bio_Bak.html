Bio: Yvonne Bodell, MM, NCIDQ

Yvonne Bodell MM, NCIDQ is the owner and principal designer of Intra Space Design. She has over 30 years of experience in residential and commercial design; including trade shows, showrooms, healthcare, corporate, and financial facilities. She has completed projects throughout the United States and in Canada. Yvonne has received a degree in Interior Design from Kendall School of Art & Design and a Masters of Management degree from Aquinas College in Grand Rapids, MI.
